Frequently Asked Questions

After you submit your quote request, our team will review your order and provide a detailed quote within 24 business hours. The quote will include pricing for the products, decoration, and shipping.

Once you receive the quote, you can accept it to place your order, or request adjustments if needed.

We offer several decoration methods to customize your apparel:

  • Screen Printing: Ideal for larger quantities and vibrant, durable designs.
  • Embroidery: Professional and long-lasting, perfect for polos, jackets, and hats.
  • Heat Transfer: Great for smaller orders, photo-quality designs, or items with complex details.
  • DTG (Direct-to-Garment): High-quality digital printing, excellent for complex designs with many colors.

Our experts will recommend the best method based on your specific needs and budget.

To help us provide the most accurate quote, please include:

  • Required delivery date
  • Description of artwork for decoration (or indicate if you'll provide art files)
  • Decoration locations (front, back, sleeve, etc.)
  • Any size breakdowns (if ordering specific quantities of each size)
  • Special shipping requirements
  • Any budget constraints

Minimum order quantities vary by decoration method:

  • Screen Printing: 12 pieces minimum per design
  • Embroidery: 6 pieces minimum
  • Heat Transfer: No minimum
  • DTG: No minimum

For larger quantities, we offer volume discounts which will be reflected in your quote.
